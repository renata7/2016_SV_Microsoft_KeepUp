Study Resources

Khan Academy is a non-profit educational organization created in 2006 by educator Salman "Sal" Khan. Salman Khan's purpose for creating this website is to provide reliable and free education for anyone, anywhere. He provides educational videos on an endless amount of subjects.

Quizlet is a great study tool that allows users to view and study from over 20 million quiz sets. Users also have the option to easily and quickly create your own sets. Quizlet allows for an easy and quick way to study. Study easily anywhere by downloading the app and making an account!

Hack My Study helps students and learners around the world achieve maximum academic and life success by teaching the best study hacks and learning techniquess. Some of the tips the site offers include learning to take good notes, beat procrastination, and overcome stress. The site addresses critical concerns of any student or learner, and helps users to overcome learning obstacles and improve as a learner.
